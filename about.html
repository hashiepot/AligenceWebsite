Welcome To Aligence

Your Intergalactic Guide to Knowledge!

At AliGence, we believe learning should be as exciting as it is educational. We created AliGence to transform science education by merging interactive technology with engaging, hands-on learning. Designed specifically for Grade 7 students at Malabon City National Science and Mathematics High School, our platform uses a blend of AI-driven chatbot support and 2D pet simulation to make science concepts come alive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make science accessible, engaging, and personalized. Through interactive lessons and an AI-guided experience, AliGence helps students grasp complex concepts with ease, allowing every learner to progress at their own pace. By shifting mobile device usage from distraction to education, AliGence fosters curiosity and learning within a fun, game-like environment.

Why AliGence?

AliGence was created to address the unique challenges of science education. Traditional methods sometimes fall short of capturing student interest or adapting to their varied levels of understanding. AliGence’s AI chatbot offers a personalized approach, providing guidance and support tailored to each student’s learning journey. Our virtual pet simulation keeps students engaged, turning science exploration into an interactive adventure.

Our Team Members

At AliGence, our team is united by a shared mission: to make science education engaging, interactive, and accessible. We are educators, developers, designers, and innovators working together to create a fun and impactful learning experience for students. Meet the minds behind AliGence!
